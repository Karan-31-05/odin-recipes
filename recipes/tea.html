Tea

Description

Tasty tea for refresing the mood.edfredfregredfredfredfrededrderedfredrfesdfrerf

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 cup nonfat dry milk powder
  • 1 ½ cups unsweetened instant tea

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the sugar, ginger, milk powder, and tea.
  2. Store in an airtight container.
  3. To serve, mix 2 tablespoons of the tea mixture with 1 cup of hot water.
